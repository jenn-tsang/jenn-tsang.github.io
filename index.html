Smart Kitchen

At WicHacks, a 24-hour hackathon, my team and I designed a Smart Kitchen tool, a hands-free projection device, to project recipes onto any kitchen surface to avoid the need to scroll through recipes. This resolves the issue of cross-contamination of foods. We created a food recipe using JavaScript, CSS, HTML, and Bootstrap to demonstrate the use of our product.
